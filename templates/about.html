About Us

Welcome to CourtMetrics Dashboard, a project developed as part of the CS_5934 course, under the guidance of Professor Soheil Sibdari. This initiative is hosted by the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech.

About the Department

This project is part of the capstone initiative by the Department of Computer Science, Virginia Tech. The department is dedicated to advancing knowledge in computer science through education, research, and innovation.

Our Mission

At CourtMetrics, our mission is to revolutionize tennis analytics by leveraging advanced machine learning models, data visualization techniques, and real-time insights. We aim to provide actionable analytics for players, coaches, and analysts to enhance their understanding of match dynamics and player performance.
